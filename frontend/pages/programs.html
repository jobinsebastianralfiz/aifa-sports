OUR PROGRAMS

Structured training programs for every age and skill level

At AIFA Football Academy, we offer comprehensive training programs designed to develop players at every stage of their football journey. From young beginners taking their first steps to advanced players preparing for competitive football, our programs are tailored to maximize potential.

Ages 5-8

LITTLE CHAMPIONS

Our foundational program introduces young children to the beautiful game through fun, engaging activities. We focus on developing motor skills, coordination, and a love for football in a supportive environment.

  • Basic ball control and coordination
  • Fun games and activities
  • Social skills and teamwork
  • Introduction to basic rules
Duration
60 mins
Sessions
3x Week

JUNIOR STARS

Building on fundamentals, this program introduces more structured training with emphasis on technical skills, tactical awareness, and competitive play. Players begin to understand positions and team dynamics.

  • Technical skill development
  • Position-specific training
  • Small-sided competitive games
  • Basic tactical understanding

ELITE YOUTH
